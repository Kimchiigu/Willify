What is Willify?

Willify is a renowned music streaming platform that made its debut in 2006. Originating as a digital audio service, Willify quickly gained prominence and has become a household name in the world of music. Starting with a vision to revolutionize how people consume music, Willify has grown into a global phenomenon with millions of active users.

Our History

Timeline of Willify's Journey

2006

In October 2006, Willify was founded and launched its digital audio service, providing users with a basic library of songs. Early features included a simple user interface and basic search functionality.

2008

Willify introduced its first major update, significantly expanding its music library and improving the user interface. The platform also launched its first mobile app, enabling users to stream music on the go.

2010

Willify rolled out its groundbreaking algorithm for personalized playlists, offering curated music recommendations based on users' listening habits. The platform celebrated reaching 10 million active users, marking a significant growth milestone.

2015

Willify expanded its services to over 50 countries, making it a truly global music streaming platform. The introduction of high-definition audio streaming options and the addition of podcasts and exclusive artist interviews further diversified its content.
